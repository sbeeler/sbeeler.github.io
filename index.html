Suzy Beeler

Random walking my way to a PhD in Biology

I’m a 6th-year graduate student in Rob Phillips’ group at Caltech. In the lab, I bring biophysical models to bear on questions of gene regulation and the co-evolution of transcription factors and their binding sites. In the classroom, I enjoy the opportunity to expose students to the value of approaching biological inquiry from computational, mathematical, and physical perspectives. Outside of my academic duties, I enjoy meeting other people’s cats and trying out new board and card games.
